Plain Rice

View all recipes

Description

Plain Rice is a simple yet essential dish enjoyed in cuisines around the world. Light, fluffy, and mildly fragrant,
it serves as the perfect base for countless meals, from rich stews and curries to stir-fries and grilled dishes. Its neutral flavor allows it to complement both savory and spicy foods, making it
a versatile staple in the kitchen. With just rice, water, and a pinch of salt, you can prepare a satisfying and wholesome side dish that never goes out of style.

Cooked to perfection, plain rice has a tender but slightly firm texture, with each grain separate rather than sticky.
The secret lies in rinsing the rice before cooking to remove excess starch and allowing it to rest after boiling so it steams to finish cooking evenly.
Whether served fresh from the pot or reheated the next day, plain rice remains a comforting and dependable part of any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • ½ tsp salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Add rice, water, and salt to a pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  4. Cover and cook for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Turn off heat and let rest 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff with a fork and serve.
