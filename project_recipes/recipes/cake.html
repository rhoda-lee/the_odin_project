Chocolate Cake

View all recipes

Description

This simple chocolate cake is a rich, moist, and fluffy treat that’s perfect for any occasion. It’s made with cocoa powder for a deep chocolate flavor, balanced with just the right amount of sweetness. Whether you enjoy it plain, dusted with powdered sugar, or topped with creamy frosting, it’s a crowd-pleaser that works just as well for birthdays as it does for an everyday dessert. The batter comes together quickly, making it a great recipe even for beginner bakers.

Baked until tender and airy, this cake has a melt-in-your-mouth texture that pairs beautifully with chocolate, vanilla, or even coffee-flavored frosting. The addition of boiling water in the batter ensures a smooth, silky consistency, giving the cake its signature moist crumb. Serve it with a scoop of ice cream for an indulgent treat, or enjoy it with a hot cup of coffee or tea for a comforting snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat until smooth.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into pans and bake 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes in the pan, then transfer to a wire rack.
  7. Frost when completely cooled.
